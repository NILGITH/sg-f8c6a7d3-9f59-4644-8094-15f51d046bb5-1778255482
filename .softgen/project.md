# Festival des Grillades d'Abidjan

## Vision
Showcase website for Abidjan's premier grilled food festival, celebrating West African culinary culture with an integrated blog for news, recipes, and festival updates.

## Design
**Style Direction:** West African street food market energy - vibrant, communal, warm
**Palette:**
- `--background: 40 35% 97%` (warm cream #fef5e7)
- `--foreground: 0 0% 10%` (charcoal #1a1a1a)
- `--primary: 14 100% 60%` (flame orange #ff6b35)
- `--secondary: 0 65% 51%` (ember red #d32f2f)
- `--accent: 30 80% 55%` (golden spice)
- `--muted: 30 20% 85%` (light smoke)

**Typography:**
- Display: Fraunces (700) for headlines
- Body: Source Serif 4 (400, 600)

## Features
- Festival homepage with hero, about, program highlights
- Integrated blog with article listings and individual post pages
- Contact/ticketing information
- Photo gallery section
